The People Problem

The tear between artists and developers

Who does the piece belong to and the legalities of AI Art?

    For AI Art
  • People who are unskilled can generate their own prompt out!
  • It's very entertaining to play around with
  • You can get a free image to use!
  • Currently, it's unlikely for AI Art to replace the versatility of a human for requesting a very specific piece.
    Against AI Art
  • When AI technology gets better, there will always be grief in human replacement of that work. Art is no different.
  • Additionally, many of these services are free to use so the question becomes: When will AI become more efficient than hiring people to do Art?
  • If AI samples off an artist's style, is that legal for the developers to just use that without permission?
  • What if AI are able to completely replicate famous, and expensive paintins? It might encourage fraud.
